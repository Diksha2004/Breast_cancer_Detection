💗 Breast Cancer Prevention & Self-Care 💗

💡 How to Prevent Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer prevention starts with a healthy lifestyle. Follow these steps to reduce your risk:

  • 🎗️ Maintain a healthy weight and avoid obesity.
  • 🎗️ Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes a day.
  • 🎗️ Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • 🎗️ Limit alcohol intake to lower risk factors.
  • 🎗️ Quit smoking to prevent cell damage.
  • 🎗️ Get regular check-ups & screenings for early detection.

💖 Self-Care Tips for Women

Taking care of your mind & body is equally important. Here are some self-care habits:

  • 🌸 Perform monthly breast self-exams for early detection.
  • 🌸 Reduce stress with yoga, meditation, or engaging hobbies.
  • 🌸 Stay hydrated and maintain a nutritious diet.
  • 🌸 Get enough sleep to strengthen immunity.
  • 🌸 Join support groups for awareness and encouragement.
